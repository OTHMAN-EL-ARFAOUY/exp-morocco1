Travel Philosophy


Recognized by Condé Nast Traveler as a premier specialist in the region, Experience Morocco embodies the ancient Moroccan tradition of *diyafa*—the honored art of gracious hospitality. We craft journeys that honor Morocco's rich tapestry of Berber heritage, Islamic architecture, and artisanal mastery, inviting you to connect deeply with our land's soul. Beyond tourism, we create transformative experiences that celebrate the warmth of our culture and the authenticity of our people. Our dedicated team of local guides, craftsmen, and storytellers ensures every moment reflects the true spirit of Morocco—where attention to detail, genuine connection, and seamless execution turn a journey into a cherished memory.

OUR OFFERINGS

Fixed Departures

Our monthly caravans invite you to journey through Morocco's storied landscapes in intimate gatherings of no more than ten travelers. These carefully crafted 8-night expeditions weave through the blue medinas of Chefchaouen, the earthen kasbahs of the Atlas Mountains, the timeless villages of the Sahara, and the coastal charm of our Mediterranean shores. Whether traveling solo, with companions, or seeking new friendships, you'll experience authentic Morocco—guided by local storytellers and experts—at an accessible pace and price. Departing the first Saturday of each month, these journeys await your step onto our ancient paths.

DESTINATIONS

Atlas Mountains

Full of authentic Berber villages where practices have remained the same for hundreds of years, stunning mountain vistas, and crisp air-- a trip to the Atlas region makes for the perfect tranquil escape into nature.

Highlights: Hiking in Imlil, Tea with a local Berber family, Lunch overlooking the mountains

DESTINATIONS

Marrakech

At the base of the Atlas Mountains, Marrakech is a mesmerizing city unlike any other. From the bustling Medina, ever-growing art scene, and vibrant nightlife, this city offers something for everyone and is not to be missed.

Highlights: Medina and souks tour, Majorelle Gardens, Tagine Cooking Class

DESTINATIONS

Casablanca

Tradition and urban life collide in the economic hub of Morocco. From the Art Deco district to the thriving nightlife-- Casablanca is an urban metropolis brimming with energy.

Highlights: Hassan II Mosque, Rick’s Cafe

DESTINATIONS

Chefchaouen

Chefchaouen, or The Blue City, is a photogenic gem tucked within the Rif mountains known for its winding streets and powder blue walls.

Highlights: Blue City Tour & Photoshoot

DESTINATIONS

Essaouira

The coastal views of Essaouira make for a relaxed escape unlike any other. Situated on Morocco’s Atlantic coast, the easygoing and charming spirit of Essaouira embodies the feeling of a calm paradise.

Highlights: Seafood lunch at the harbor, Kitesurf lesson

DESTINATIONS

Fes

A visit to Fes is a visit back in time. As the world’s only intact medieval city, the narrow alleyways of Fes allow a glimpse into the past as you to experience the sights, sounds and smells of the souk and learn about rich history of Morocco’s cultural capital.

Highlights: Tour of Morocco's oldest Medina, Tasting Tour of local food vendors

DESTINATIONS

Ouarzazate

Known as the gateway to the Sahara, the impressive kasbahs, or ancient fortresses, of Ouarzazate have been the the backdrop for many films over the years.

Highlights: ATV Ride near Kasbah Ben Ait Haddou, cinema studios of “Morocco’s Hollywood”

DESTINATIONS

Rabat

Rebuilt at the start of the 20th century, Rabat is synonymous with contemporary Morocco. The capital city features the distinct architectural style of modern Morocco, while being home to several important monuments that continue to preserve the nation’s history.

Highlights: Tea at Cafe Maure, Chellah Necropolis, Hassan Tower

DESTINATIONS

Tangier

Situated at the crossroads of the Atlantic and Mediterranean, it’s no surprise that Tangier’s breathtaking views and laid back atmosphere have inspired artists from Matisse to the Rolling Stones.

Highlights: Tea with a view at Cafe Haffa, Hercules Cave

DESTINATIONS

The Sahara Desert

A destination you’ll have to see to believe-- few experiences are more special than an overnight in the red sand of the Saharan dunes. From a sunset camel ride, fireside musical performances, stargazing, and even seeing the Milky Way in the desert sky, the Sahara is a must-see when in Morocco.

Highlights: Sunset Camel Ride, Campfire dinner, Sunrise Sandboarding
